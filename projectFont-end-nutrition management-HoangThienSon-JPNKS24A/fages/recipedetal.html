Cooking method

Describe how to prepare this recipe

  1. Heat the oven to 200C/fan 180C/gas 6. Put the cauliflower in an ovenproof dish or tin, add the turmeric, some seasoning and 2 tbsp oil, and toss together. Roast for 20-25 minutes until tender. Tip onto a plate and cool.

    Meanwhile boil the rice in salted water until tender, adding the edamame for the last minute of cooking. Drain and leave to cool. Leave to cool, then toss together the herbs and rice.

    Mix together the dressing ingredients. Divide the cauliflower and rice between plates, scatter over the cashews and drizzle with the dressing.
